Logistics

The Internet Archive is located at 300 Funston Avenue in the Richmond District of San Francisco, four miles west of downtown. Our neighborhood, bordered by the Presidio to the north and Golden Gate Park to the south, is a melting pot of cultures with a plethora of eateries, shops, and bookstores. The building itself, formerly a Christian Science church, now houses petabytes of data — including images, texts, music, software and more.

Recommended Accommodations – Near The Mint

InterContinental

 The InterContinental San Francisco is a luxury hotel, located within easy walking distance of The Mint, Union Square, premium shopping areas, a diverse collection of trendy and upscale restaurants and bars, and a number of major San Francisco tourist attractions.

888 Howard Street, San Francisco
See Map

Pickwick Hotel

 The historical landmark Pickwick Hotel, located directly across the street from The Mint, offers affordable stylish and sophisticated accommodations. Enjoy a drink at their intimate SOMA Restaurant & Bar, treat yourself in one of their three restaurants, or choose from dozens of restaurants, or activities that surround the hotel.

85 5th Street, San Francisco
See Map

Good Hotel

 Dedicated to a more eco-friendly experience, the Good Hotel is proof that living green and living well can go hand in hand. This slightly more budget-conscious hotel is located a few short blocks away from The Mint. Free wifi is provided in all rooms and social areas, coffee & tea served fresh each morning, you will have access to a heated outdoor pool and fitness room at their sister hotel across the street, and complimentary bicycles for exploring San Francisco are provided.

112 7th Street, San Francisco
